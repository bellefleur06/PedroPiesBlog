About Me

Greetings, my name is PedroPies. I am a huge fan of Japanese culture, especially anime, manga, and Japanese dramas and movies. For years, I've been watching and reading this type of media, and I've developed a keen sense of what makes a good story and what makes a bad one.

I enjoy sharing my thoughts and opinions with others who share my interests, so I decided to start a blog to share my reviews with the rest of the world. I am here to offer my insights and recommendations, whether you are a fan of the latest hit anime series or a long-time devotee of classic manga.

"Join me on my journey as I discover the best of Japanese entertainment."
